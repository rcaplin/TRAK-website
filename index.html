About Us

The Textile Recycling Association of Kenya (TRAK) is building the ecosystem for textile circularity in East Africa. We aim to reduce textile waste, extend the life of garments, and build a responsible, transparent system that supports people and the planet.

What We Do

  • Advocate for policies that support textile recycling and extended producer responsibility (EPR)
  • Connect and support stakeholders across the textile value chain
  • Promote public awareness of textile waste and sustainable fashion
  • Collect and share data to guide action in Kenya’s circular textile transition
