About Us

TRAK is building the ecosystem for textile circularity in East Africa. We aim to reduce textile waste, extend the life of garments, and build a responsible, transparent system that supports people and the planet.

Our Programs

  • Policy advocacy around textile recycling and extended producer responsibility (EPR)
  • Connecting and supporting stakeholders across the textile value chain
  • Public awareness campaigns on textile waste and sustainable fashion
  • Data collection and sharing to guide Kenya’s textile circularity transition
