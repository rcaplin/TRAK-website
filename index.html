About TRAK

The Textile Recycling Association of Kenya (TRAK) is a pioneering organization dedicated to transforming the textile industry in East Africa through sustainable practices and circular economy principles.

Our mission is to reduce textile waste, create sustainable employment opportunities, and establish Kenya as a global leader in textile recycling and upcycling innovation.

Through collaboration with local communities, international partners, and policy makers, we're building a more sustainable future for fashion, one thread at a time.
